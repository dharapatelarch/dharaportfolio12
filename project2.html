The Lesiure Pod

Concept : A compact leisure space where two opposing walls frame a central water feature, creating a balance between built form and openness. The open sides maintain visual connectivity, natural ventilation, and daylight, forming a calm and comfortable environment for relaxation and casual interaction.

Within a 10 m × 10 m site, two opposing walls frame the space while the other sides remain open for connectivity. A central water feature becomes the focal point, allowing natural light, ventilation, and a calm setting for relaxation.

Site Area : 100 m 2

The construction system follows a clear sequence from foundation to slab, ensuring structural stability within the compact site. Masonry walls, beams, and slab form a durable framework while maintaining the intended spatial layout.
