The Flow Park

Concept : This landscape design works well next to a mall by acting as a green buffer that reduces noise, heat, and visual clutter from the urban surroundings. The open lawns and plazas serve as spill-out spaces for shoppers, while shaded paths, water features, and activity nodes provide relaxation and smooth pedestrian movement..

Site Location : Behind Inorbit Mall , Vadodara Site Area : 23,000 m 2

This landscape design features an organic layout with curving pathways that guide movement through green lawns and activity zones. Circular paths and a central maze create visual and interactive focal points.
